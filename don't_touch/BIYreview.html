BABA IS YOU REVIEW

BABA IS YOU IS A CHALLENGING PUZZLE GAME THAT FEATURES THE CHARACTERS BABA, KEKE, ME, FOFO, JIJI, IT, AND ANNI (SHE'S ONLY IN 1 LEVEL THOUGH). THE GAME ALSO HAS 3 LEVEL PACKS, AND THE MAIN ONE (CALLED "BABA") LOOKS SIMPLE AT FIRST, BUT IF YOU TRANSFORM LEVEL 9 OF MAP INTO BABA AND LEVEL 11 INTO FLAG YOU CAN WIN MAP, LEADING TO ???, WHERE MAP IS JUST A LEVEL.

??? IS EXTEMLY COMPLICATED AND NOT RECCOMENDED IF YOU'RE NOT SMART ENOUGH. WINNING ??? TAKES YOU TO NULL WHERE ??? IS THE ONLY LEVEL THERE. NULL USES THE MAIN MENU THEME BUT REVERSED, GIVING IT THE STRANGE-SOUNDING MUSIC APPERENTLY CALLED "FUN.OGG"

IN MAP THERE'S 13 SUB-WORLDS, IN ??? THERE ARE 3, AND IN NULL THERE'S ONLY 1. BABA APPEARS IN MOST LEVELS, EVEN IN THE MUSEUM AND NEW ADVENTURES LEVEL PACKS AND NOT JUST BABA LEVEL PACK. 3D ONLY APPEARS IN 6 LEVELS, HIDDEN IN 2 OF THEM. A LOT OF OBJECTS DON'T APPEAR IN ANY LEVEL, ONLY IN THE LEVER EDITOR. ANNI ONLY APPEARS IN 1 LEVEL AND ISN'T IN THE LEVEL EDITOR FOR SOME REASON.

OVERALL I WOULD SAY BABA IS YOU IS GOOD, WITH SO MANY CHALLENGING LEVELS AND THE LEVEL EDITOR, THE LEVEL EDITOR HAS SO MANY POSSIBILITIES AS THERES MORE MUSIC, MORE THEMES, MORE SIZES (MAXIMUM 30 BY 18), MORE OBJECTS, MORE PROPERTIES, AND MORE WORDS OVERALL.
